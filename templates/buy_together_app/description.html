buyTogether

The idea of the project is to let group of people to organize together and buy straight from the supplier at lower cost.

Goals
- to create simple and easy communication between an organized group of people and a supplier.
- lower the prices of food cost
Our plan. In order to execute our idea, we intend to build a web application, we will divide our work between us(five members total) and each will be responsible for each section of the project.
